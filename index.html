About me

Hi, I am a Multimedia Graphic Designer from Athens, Greece. I have knowledge in digital image, video, and audio processing, as well as in motion graphics. Additionally, I have skills in designing and implementing responsive static websites. In my free time, I enjoy cartoon sketch and 2d character animation with Anime Studio Pro Software, as well as the development 2d mini video games with Construct 2 Software. My favorite retro console they are Gameboy from Nintendo, and my personal retro computer is Amiga 500 from Commodore.
